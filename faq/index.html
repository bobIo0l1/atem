FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can you bring food or drinks inside of the escape room?
A: Drinks are allowed if they are covered in order to prevent spills. Food is not permitted in the escape room.

Q: Is there a limit to how many people there can be in a group?
A: Yes there is of 6 people per escape room.

Q: Can you do the escape room solo?
A: Yes you can but the it will be harder becuase there is only one person doing it.

Q: What will happen if something is broken?
A: Unless it is essential, things will continue. If it is, then we will pause the escape room until we can repair it. Please report anything you thing is faulty to your room operator.
